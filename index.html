Bridging the Technical-Regulatory Divide

The Algorithmic Biosecurity Forum (abf.) is an interdisciplinary nexus dedicated to defining the necessary guardrails for the safe deployment of predictive models in pharmaceutical R&D. We exist to bridge the profound safety gap between technical innovation—such as GNN-driven toxicity prediction—and regulatory reality.

The forum serves as a platform for expert deliberation, curating high-impact research dispatches, white papers, and strategic dialogues intended to secure India's sovereign pharmaceutical infrastructure and set the international benchmark for intelligent, accountable drug discovery.
